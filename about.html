A small group of believers trying to change the world.

Founded in 2019 in Mexico City. 4,500+ companies formed. This is just the beginning.

“A revolution is not an event. It is a process. And it takes time.”

Our dream is to turn Latin America into a global powerhouse. We believe the engine of change is not in politics or multinational corporations — it is in entrepreneurs like you.

We were born in 2019 in a small office in Mexico City with one idea: make it radically easier for international brands to build real businesses in Mexico. Not shortcuts. Not workarounds. Real companies, fully owned by the people who built them.

Since then, we have formed over 4,500 companies through our platform. Entrepreneurs from the United States, China, Korea, Brazil, Spain, Vietnam, Argentina, and 20+ more countries have trusted us to be their strategic partner.

This is only the beginning.
