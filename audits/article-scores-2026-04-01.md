### Composite Score: **89.2 / 100**

### Top 3 Strengths
1. **Comprehensive structure and visual variety** -- 10 sections, 4 tables, 6 step boxes, multiple callout types, TOC. This is a genuine pillar page that serves both skimmers and deep readers.
2. **E-E-A-T signals deeply embedded** -- Real data points (4,500+ companies, $54.4B market, 34% YoY), named case study (GROWVE with specific week-by-week outcomes), real regulatory entity references throughout.
3. **Technical SEO near-flawless** -- All three JSON-LD schemas (Article, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList), complete OG + Twitter tags, GTM, canonical, robots, `<time>` element. This page is machine-readable and rich-snippet ready.

### Top 3 Weaknesses
1. **Mid-article CTA placement is too late** -- CRO guidelines specify a pricing anchor at 30-40% of the article. The first in-article CTA appears at roughly 60%. Readers who bounce at the midpoint never see a conversion opportunity.
2. **External source data not hyperlinked** -- IMARC Group and Statista are cited by name but not linked. This weakens E-E-A-T and misses an opportunity for outbound authority signals.
3. **No risk reversal language near CTAs** -- CRO best practices require "free assessment, no obligation" or "15-minute call" near conversion points. The closing CTA says "no commitment required" but the mid-article CTA lacks any risk reversal.

### Top 3 Quick Wins
1. **Add a mid-article CTA with pricing anchor at the 30-40% mark** (after the Requirements section, around line 600). Include "$588/month" and "free assessment" language. Expected impact: +5-10% scroll-to-CTA click rate.
2. **Hyperlink IMARC Group and Statista citations** to their source reports. Two additions, 5 minutes of work. Strengthens E-E-A-T and external link profile.
3. **Front-load the keyword in the OG title** -- Change from "The Complete Guide to Selling on Amazon Mexico in 2026" to "Sell on Amazon Mexico in 2026: The Complete Guide" to match the H1 pattern and SEO guideline.

### Top 3 Weaknesses

1. **Add `<time>` element** -- Replace `<span>March 30, 2026</span>` with `<time datetime="2026-03-30">March 30, 2026</time>`. One-line fix, immediate technical SEO improvement.
2. **Add primary keyword to 2 H2 headings** -- For example, change "The Formation Process: Week by Week" to "How to Form a Company in Mexico: Week by Week" and "How Much Does It Cost?" to "How Much Does It Cost to Form a Company in Mexico?" These changes improve keyword placement without hurting readability.
3. **Add 2-3 external authority links** -- Link COFEPRIS mentions to gob.mx/cofepris, link Secretaria de Economia to gob.mx/se, and link Amazon Seller Central Mexico to sellercentral.amazon.com.mx. Three additions, 5 minutes of work.
