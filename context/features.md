# Tally Global Services & Features

This document outlines Tally Global's services, pricing, differentiators, and key messaging to inform content creation that drives lead conversions.

## Core Value Propositions

### 1. **Entity Formation (S de RL, SA, SAPI)**
- **Service**: Full Mexican company formation including articles of incorporation, notarization, Public Commerce Registry (RPC) registration, RFC, eFirma, and legal representative appointment.
- **Benefit**: You own a fully operational Mexican legal entity in 4-6 weeks, with the right structure for your business model (S de RL de CV for most brands, SA for publicly traded aspirations, SAPI for VC-backed startups with governance needs).
- **Conversion Angle**: "Form your Mexican company in 4-6 weeks. You own the entity, we handle the bureaucracy. Entity formation is included in every Tally plan."

### 2. **COFEPRIS & Product Compliance**
- **Service**: Full COFEPRIS registration for cosmetics, supplements, food, beverages, and medical devices. Includes dossier preparation, label review, regulatory submission, and follow-up with the agency.
- **Benefit**: Navigate Mexico's health regulatory agency (equivalent of FDA) without delays or rejections. Tally has 100% first-pass approval rates and reviews ~25 labels per month, 5x faster than traditional firms.
- **Conversion Angle**: "100% first-pass COFEPRIS approval rate. Cosmetics in 3-15 days, supplements in 3-6 months. We handle the regulatory complexity so you can focus on selling."

### 3. **Imports & Customs**
- **Service**: Padron de Importadores registration, tariff classification, customs broker coordination, NOM labeling compliance, first shipment management, and ongoing import support.
- **Benefit**: Import legally under your own entity (T1 customs entry) with proper duty classification. USMCA products may qualify for 0% duty. Eliminates dependency on IOR services.
- **Conversion Angle**: "Import under your own name. T1 customs entry, proper tariff classification, and USMCA duty advantages. No more paying 19% courier surcharges."

### 4. **Banking & Finance**
- **Service**: Mexican bank account opening (MXN + USD), full KYC support, payment infrastructure setup, and ongoing banking relationship management.
- **Benefit**: Receive Amazon/Mercado Libre payouts, pay suppliers, manage duties, and repatriate profits through your own Mexican bank account in 2-4 weeks.
- **Conversion Angle**: "Your own Mexican bank account in 2-4 weeks. MXN and USD. Full KYC handled."

### 5. **Accounting & Tax Compliance**
- **Service**: Monthly bookkeeping, ISR (income tax) and IVA (value-added tax) filings, CFDI electronic invoicing, annual tax returns, and fiscal compliance monitoring.
- **Benefit**: Stay compliant with SAT requirements, recover IVA on imports (the 16% IVA is not a sunk cost), and maintain clean books for investors or auditors.
- **Conversion Angle**: "Monthly accounting from $588/mo. ISR, IVA, CFDI, and SAT compliance handled by our Mexico City team. IVA recovery on imports included."

### 6. **Marketplace Access (Amazon, Mercado Libre, Walmart)**
- **Service**: Amazon Seller Central Mexico setup, Mercado Libre seller account setup, Walmart Mexico onboarding, listing creation, and launch support.
- **Benefit**: Sell on Mexico's top ecommerce platforms under your own entity and brand. The same infrastructure works across all platforms.
- **Conversion Angle**: "Launch on Amazon Mexico, Mercado Libre, and Walmart Mexico under your own brand. One entity, three marketplaces, full ownership."

### 7. **Trademark Registration (IMPI)**
- **Service**: Mexican trademark search, IMPI (Instituto Mexicano de la Propiedad Industrial) filing, prosecution, and registration.
- **Benefit**: Protect your brand in Mexico before competitors register your name. Required for Amazon Brand Registry in Mexico.
- **Conversion Angle**: "Register your trademark with IMPI before someone else does. Required for Amazon Brand Registry in Mexico."

### 8. **Immigration**
- **Service**: Work visa applications, temporary and permanent residency, FM3 processing, and relocation support.
- **Benefit**: Get legal residency and work authorization in Mexico for founders or key employees who need to be on the ground.
- **Conversion Angle**: "Work visas and residency permits for founders and key team members relocating to Mexico."

### 9. **China Desk**
- **Service**: Mandarin-speaking support for Chinese exporters. Entity formation, banking, customs, and marketplace setup with full language and cultural support.
- **Benefit**: Eliminate the 19% courier import surcharge by forming a Mexican entity and importing directly. Access Amazon Mexico and Mercado Libre without intermediaries.
- **Conversion Angle**: "Mandarin-speaking team. Eliminate the 19% courier surcharge. Entity, banking, imports, and marketplace access, all in your language."

- **Tally: End-to-end market entry**. Passport only handles cross-border shipping.
- **Tally: Entity, banking, compliance, imports**. Passport provides none of these.
- **Tally: You are the importer of record**. With Passport, you are not legally present in Mexico.

- **Tally: Build long-term value**. MoR arrangements create dependency, not equity.
- **Tally: Lower total cost at scale**. MoR fees compound; ownership costs flatten.

- **Tally: 8-12 weeks to first sale**. DIY takes 12-18 months.
- **Tally: One vendor, one point of contact**. DIY means juggling lawyer + accountant + customs broker + bank + regulatory consultant.
- **Tally: 3x lower total cost**. DIY legal mistakes, delays, and redundant vendor fees compound.
- **Tally: 4,500+ companies of experience**. DIY means learning from your own expensive mistakes.
