## Internal Linking Best Practices

1. **3-6 links per article**: Minimum 3, optimal 4-6, max 8 for long-form
2. **Link naturally**: Only when genuinely relevant and helpful to the reader
3. **Vary anchor text**: Use different phrases for the same destination across articles
4. **Descriptive anchors only**: Never "click here," "read more," or "learn more"
5. **Early links carry weight**: Place important links in the first third of the article
6. **Max 2 links per paragraph**: Do not cluster links
7. **Always link to /#cta-final**: Every article needs at least one CTA link
8. **Deep linking preferred**: Link to specific service pages, not just the homepage
