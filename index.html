Since January 2025, Mexico's SAT requires all foreign Amazon sellers to have a legal representative in Mexico. That means you need: a Mexican legal entity, an RFC (tax ID), an eFirma (digital signature), a Mexican bank account, and depending on your products, COFEPRIS permits or NOM certifications. We handle every single one of these as your strategic partner. You focus on your product — we handle Mexico.

For products without COFEPRIS requirements — general merchandise, electronics, clothing — you can go from zero to selling in 8-12 weeks. Entity formation takes 4-6 weeks, banking runs in parallel at 2-4 weeks, first import at 2-3 weeks, then marketplace setup.

Products that need COFEPRIS sanitary registration (supplements, cosmetics, medical devices) add 3-18 months for permit approval. But we start that process immediately alongside everything else, so no time is wasted.
