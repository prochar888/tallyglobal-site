Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

1. Data Controller

Tally.Legal S.A.P.I. de C.V. (“Tally”), with registered address at Calle Xola 1106, Col. Narvarte Poniente, Benito Juárez, Mexico City, C.P. 03020, Mexico, is responsible for collecting, using, and protecting your personal data.

The confidentiality and proper protection of personal data entrusted to Tally is of the utmost importance to us. Tally is committed to handling your personal data responsibly and in compliance with Mexico’s Federal Law for the Protection of Personal Data Held by Private Parties (“LFPDPPP”), its Regulations, and all applicable regulations.

2. Transparency in Data Handling

Our primary objective is to provide a reliable, secure, and streamlined corporate formation and market entry coordination service through our platform and network of independent Strategic Allies (notaries, accountants, customs brokers, regulatory specialists, and financial institutions). To this end, we use technologies such as data analytics to process your personal data with greater precision, in order to fulfill the primary purposes set forth below. All technology used to process your information meets the highest standards of information security.

3. Security Measures

Your personal data is protected by administrative, physical, and technical security measures designed to prevent loss, alteration, destruction, unauthorized use, access, or disclosure. The platform stores user information under security standards that exceed those required by applicable legislation, and data is always stored in encrypted form.

4. Purposes of Data Collection

Your personal data may be used for any or all of the following purposes:

Primary Purposes (essential for service delivery):

  • Transmitting necessary information to our Strategic Allies to facilitate the provision of their independent professional services.
  • Creating statistical profiles for continuous service improvement.
  • Personalizing the information and services offered to you through the platform.
  • Commercial prospecting related to our services.
  • Processing ARCO rights requests (Access, Rectification, Cancellation, and Opposition).

If you do not wish for Tally to process your personal data, or if you wish to further limit the use or disclosure thereof, please send an email to hola@tally.legal with your full name. You may exercise your ARCO rights at any time.

5. How We Obtain Your Personal Data

We may collect your personal data when you use any of our services through our website or other channels declared by Tally, without objecting to the processing of your personal data.

6. Personal Data Collected

The personal data we may collect includes: name, email address, age, gender, location, CURP, RFC, tax identification certificate, address, date of birth, marital status, and the website consulted prior to visiting the Tally website. Additionally, images, audio, sounds, and/or videos provided during calls, video calls, or general communications with Tally.

We inform you that we do not collect patrimonial data as defined by the LFPDPPP, nor do we collect data considered sensitive personal data.

7. Recordings

In compliance with Articles 4 and 6 of the LFPDPPP, Tally informs you that virtual sessions may be recorded. Images, audio, video, and/or sounds captured during calls, video calls, and/or general communications with Tally will be used solely for informational purposes: to provide comprehensive advisory covering all requested points, and for service quality improvement by analyzing the quality of service provided by our team, which is our priority.

The user agrees that Tally and its Strategic Allies are authorized to make discreet and moderate use of any recordings for the purposes described in this notice, including after the termination of the contractual relationship, provided such use complies with good customs and morals, unless the data subject objects.

8. Data Protection

Biometric data, audio, video, and image data from recordings are protected and safeguarded and may not be used or distributed for any purpose other than those expressly described herein. Disclosure, dissemination, or duplication is prohibited, subject to the exceptions set forth in Article 37 of the LFPDPPP.

9. Transfer of Personal Data

We commit to not transferring your personal data to third parties other than those described herein without your prior consent, except for the exceptions provided in Article 37 of the LFPDPPP. Tally may disclose your personal data to Strategic Allies in connection with the services and products offered, when they need to know such data to fulfill their independent professional obligations, and always subject to the same confidentiality obligations as Tally.

10. Cookies and Web Beacons

Our website uses cookies and web beacons to monitor your behavior as an Internet user, in order to provide a better service and browsing experience. The personal data we may obtain through these technologies includes: browser used, server name, IP address, date, time, and duration of Internet and website browsing, and pages consulted before entering Tally.

These technologies may be disabled through your Internet browser by configuring the privacy section to disallow cookies from the site you visit. This procedure may vary depending on your browser.

11. ARCO Rights and Consent Revocation

At all times, you have the right to access, rectify, and cancel your personal data, as well as to oppose its processing or revoke the consent you have granted to us. To exercise your ARCO rights, please send an email to hola@tally.legal with your full name and a description of your request. We will respond within the timeframes established by the LFPDPPP.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Tally reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be published on the platform. Continued use of the Service after modifications constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

13. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your data rights, contact us at: hola@tally.legal
