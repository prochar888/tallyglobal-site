Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you use our contact forms or request a consultation. This may include:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Company name and website
  • Details about your business needs

We also collect certain information automatically through analytics tools, including your IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and time spent on our site.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide requested services
  • Follow up on consultations and service requests
  • Improve our website, content, and service offerings
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns to enhance the user experience
  • Send relevant updates about our services (only if you have opted in)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect or process data on our behalf:

  • Google Analytics (GA4) — for website traffic analysis and usage metrics. Data is processed by Google under their privacy policy.
  • Google Tag Manager (GTM) — for managing analytics and tracking scripts.
  • Formspree — for processing form submissions. Your form data is transmitted through Formspree's servers. See their privacy policy.
  • YouTube — embedded videos on some pages may load cookies from YouTube/Google when played.

4. Data Retention

Lead data submitted through our forms (name, email, company information) is retained until you request its deletion. Analytics data is retained according to the default retention settings of Google Analytics (14 months).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal data from our systems

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@tallyglobal.ai.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies primarily through Google Analytics (GA4) and Google Tag Manager. These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our site. They collect information such as:

  • Pages visited and navigation paths
  • Session duration and bounce rate
  • Referral source (how you found our site)
  • General geographic location (country/city level)

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain site functionality.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@tallyglobal.ai
  • Address: Xola 1106, Narvarte Poniente, Mexico City, 03020, Mexico
