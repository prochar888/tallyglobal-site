# SEO Cannibalization Audit: tallyglobal.ai vs tallylegal.io
## Date: March 30, 2026

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

tallylegal.io has **90+ blog posts** (mostly in Spanish) covering entity formation, taxation, imports, trademarks, compliance, startups, and finance. tallyglobal.ai has **2 blog posts** (in English) targeting Amazon/ecommerce sellers.

**The key distinction:**
- **tallylegal.io** = Mexican domestic market + LatAm entrepreneurs + Spanish-language SEO
- **tallyglobal.ai** = International sellers (US, China, Korea, etc.) + English-language SEO + marketplace/ecommerce focus

**Cannibalization risk is LOW** because of language difference, but there are **5 high-risk topic overlaps** that need cross-linking strategy.

## CROSS-LINKING STRATEGY

### Rule 1: tallyglobal.ai → tallylegal.io (when reader doesn't fit our ICP)
Every tallyglobal.ai article should include this block when relevant:

> **Not selling on marketplaces?** If you're a foreigner starting a non-ecommerce business in Mexico (tech company, consulting, restaurant, real estate), check out [Tally Legal](https://www.tallylegal.io/extranjeros) — our sister platform for general entity formation and corporate services.

### Rule 2: tallylegal.io → tallyglobal.ai (when reader is a marketplace seller)
tallylegal.io articles about imports, COFEPRIS, or foreign entity formation should link to tallyglobal.ai:

> **Selling on Amazon or Mercado Libre?** Visit [TallyGlobal.ai](https://tallyglobal.ai) for specialized marketplace entry services including FBA coordination, COFEPRIS compliance, and seller account setup.

### Rule 3: Differentiate by angle, not topic
| Topic | tallylegal.io angle | tallyglobal.ai angle |
|-------|---------------------|---------------------|
| Entity formation | General business (restaurants, tech, consulting) | Amazon/Meli sellers specifically |
| COFEPRIS | Regulatory overview for any business | Product compliance for ecommerce brands (supplements, cosmetics for FBA) |
| Imports | General import framework | Amazon FBA logistics, T1 vs IOR, door-to-FBA |
| RFC | What is RFC for Mexican entrepreneurs | RFC as Amazon requirement for foreign sellers |
| Trademarks | IMPI process for Mexican brands | Brand Registry for Amazon/Meli sellers |
| Banking | General business banking | Payoneer + Mexican bank for marketplace payouts |

### Rule 4: Language separation
- tallylegal.io = Spanish-first (with 4 English articles)
- tallyglobal.ai = English-only
- This naturally prevents most cannibalization
- The 4 English articles on tallylegal.io are the only real risk
