Imports & Customs Clearance

Test shipments via our Texas warehouse or full Importer of Record (IOR) under your entity. Tariff classification, customs broker management, Padrón de Importadores, and door-to-FBA delivery.

Two Import Schemes

Choose the right approach based on your stage and volume.

T1 — Consolidated / Test Shipment

Quick market-test imports via Tally’s warehouse in Laredo, Texas. Ideal for first-time importers or small volumes.

  • Ship to our US warehouse
  • Consolidated packing with other products
  • Rapid delivery to Amazon FBA
  • No Padrón de Importadores needed
  • Perfect for validating product-market fit
  • Limited tax deduction capability
Recommended for scale

IOR — Importer of Record

Formal importation under your own Mexican entity. Full compliance, full tax deductions, scale-ready.

  • Import under your company’s RFC
  • Full IVA and ISR tax deductions
  • Unlimited volume and frequency
  • Padrón de Importadores required (we handle it)
  • Complete pedimento documentation
  • Long-term operational foundation

What’s Included in Every Import

End-to-end management from your origin warehouse to Amazon FBA Mexico.

Tariff Classification

10-digit tariff code (fracción arancelaria) assignment to determine your exact duty rate and regulatory requirements.

Import Cost Quotation

Detailed breakdown of all costs before you ship: duties, taxes, broker fees, transport, and handling. No surprises.

Padrón de Importadores

Registration with SAT authorizing your entity to import. We also handle Padrón Sectorial for protected industries (textiles, footwear, chemicals).

NOM Labeling Compliance

Spanish-language oversticker labels meeting NOM-050 (commercial info), NOM-141 (cosmetics), and other applicable standards.

Customs Broker Management

We source, link, and manage your customs broker (agente aduanal). Encargo Conferido authorization handled on your behalf.

Document Preparation

Commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, technical data sheets — reviewed and prepared for customs.

Customs Clearance

Real-time coordination during clearance, including supervision of physical inspections (red-light “semaforo fiscal”).

Land Transport to FBA

Door-to-door delivery from customs to Amazon fulfillment centers in CDMX, Guadalajara, Monterrey, or Toluca.

USMCA-origin products from the US or Canada may qualify for 0% preferential duty rates. Proper tariff classification can significantly reduce your effective rate.
