Why Account Ownership Matters

Some services list your products on their own megastore accounts. You never own the seller account, never build brand equity, and never control pricing or inventory. If they shut down or change terms, you lose everything.

With Tally, you own your seller accounts. Your brand name, your reviews, your sales history, your advertising data — all under your control. If you ever change service providers, your marketplace presence stays with you.
