Trademark Registration (IMPI)

Register and protect your brand in Mexico through IMPI (Instituto Mexicano de la Propiedad Industrial). Prevent counterfeits, secure your IP, and unlock Brand Registry on Amazon and Mercado Libre.

IMPI

Direct trademark registration through Mexico's IP office. Protect your brand, prevent counterfeits, and unlock Amazon Brand Registry and Mercado Libre brand programs.

Why Register Your Trademark in Mexico

Prevent Counterfeits

Without a registered trademark, anyone can sell products using your brand name on Amazon MX and Mercado Libre. Registration gives you legal tools to stop them.

Amazon Brand Registry

Amazon’s Brand Registry requires a registered trademark. It unlocks A+ Content, brand analytics, and enhanced protection against listing hijackers.

Mercado Libre Brand Protection

MELI’s Brand Protection Program removes counterfeit listings. A registered trademark is your entry ticket.

IP Portfolio Building

Your Mexican trademark strengthens your global IP portfolio and may qualify for protection in other Madrid Protocol countries.

Types of Trademarks

Word Marks

Your brand name in standard text. Broadest protection.

Design Marks

Your logo or visual brand identity. Protects the graphic element.

Combined Marks

Name + logo together. Most common for ecommerce brands.

Trade Dress

Distinctive packaging or product appearance. For unique physical products.

Process Timeline

From application to registration certificate in 4–6 months.

IMPI Search

Week 1. Availability search to verify no conflicts.

Application Filing

Week 2. Formal application with IMPI.

Examination

Months 2–4. IMPI reviews and may issue office actions.

Publication

Month 4–5. Published in IMPI gazette for opposition period.

Registration

Month 5–6. Certificate issued. Valid 10 years, renewable.
