2. Services Offered

Through our platform and Strategic Allies network, Tally coordinates the following services:

  • Company Formation: Coordination of Mexican entity incorporation (S.A. de C.V., S.A.P.I. de C.V., S. de R.L. de C.V.) through licensed notaries public, including RFC registration and eFirma procurement with SAT.
  • Import Advisory: Guidance on import requirements, Padrón de Importadores registration assistance, tariff classification advisory, NOM labeling coordination, and connection with licensed customs brokers. Tally does not perform customs clearance or import operations directly.
  • Regulatory Compliance Coordination: Coordination of COFEPRIS registrations, NOM certifications, and sanitary permit applications through specialized regulatory partners.
  • Banking Coordination: Facilitation of bank account opening processes with partner financial institutions. Tally is not a financial institution and does not provide banking services.
  • Accounting & Tax Coordination: Coordination of monthly bookkeeping, tax filing (ISR, IVA, DIOT), and fiscal compliance through licensed accountants in our partner network.
  • Marketplace Onboarding: Assistance with Amazon Mexico and Mercado Libre seller account setup and configuration.
  • Trademark Coordination: Coordination of trademark registration applications through IMPI via licensed intellectual property specialists.
  • Immigration Coordination: Coordination of visa and residency applications through immigration specialists.
